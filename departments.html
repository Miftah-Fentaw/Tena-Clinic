    Neurological Sciences Department

    The Neurology Department at Tena Clinic provides expert diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of brain and nervous system disorders, including epilepsy, stroke, Parkinson's disease, and multiple sclerosis. Our neurologists use advanced imaging and monitoring technology to deliver precise, effective care.

    Brain Monitoring

    Our neurologists use continuous EEG and brain activity monitoring to detect and manage seizure disorders and other neurological conditions with precision.

    EEG Testing

    We perform comprehensive electroencephalography (EEG) testing to evaluate brain electrical activity and diagnose neurological conditions.

    Neurological Exam

    Thorough neurological examinations to assess reflexes, coordination, sensation, and cognitive function for accurate diagnosis.

    Treatment Plans

    Individualized treatment plans developed by our neurology team to manage chronic and acute neurological conditions effectively.

    Surgical Services Department

    Tena Clinic's Surgical Services Department offers a full range of general and specialized surgical procedures. Our surgeons are trained in both open and minimally invasive techniques, ensuring the safest and most effective outcomes for every patient.

    Minimally Invasive

    We specialize in laparoscopic and robotic-assisted procedures that reduce recovery time, minimize scarring, and lower the risk of complications.

    Advanced Procedures

    Our surgical team performs complex procedures using the latest technology and evidence-based protocols for optimal patient outcomes.

    Safe Operations

    Rigorous pre-operative and intra-operative safety protocols ensure every patient receives the highest standard of surgical care.

    Recovery Support

    Dedicated post-operative care and rehabilitation support to help patients recover safely and return to their daily lives as quickly as possible.

    Dental Care Department

    Tena Clinic's Dental Care Department provides comprehensive oral health services for patients of all ages. From routine cleanings and fillings to orthodontics and cosmetic dentistry, our dental team is committed to keeping your smile healthy.

    Oral Health

    Preventive and restorative oral health care including examinations, fillings, extractions, and gum disease treatment.

    Teeth Cleaning

    Professional teeth cleaning and scaling to remove plaque and tartar, preventing cavities and gum disease.

    Cosmetic Dentistry

    Teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeover procedures to enhance the appearance of your teeth and boost your confidence.

    Orthodontics

    Braces and clear aligner treatments to correct misaligned teeth and bite issues for children, teens, and adults.

    Ophthalmology Department

    Tena Clinic's Ophthalmology Department provides complete eye care services, from routine vision testing to advanced surgical treatments. Our ophthalmologists use state-of-the-art equipment to diagnose and treat a wide range of eye conditions.

    Vision Testing

    Comprehensive eye exams to assess visual acuity, detect refractive errors, and screen for conditions like glaucoma and cataracts.

    Retinal Imaging

    High-resolution retinal photography and OCT scans to detect diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and other retinal diseases.

    Laser Surgery

    LASIK and other refractive laser procedures to correct nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism for lasting clear vision.

    Eye Care Plans

    Personalized eye care management plans including prescription eyewear, medicated drops, and long-term monitoring for chronic eye conditions.

    Cardiology Department

    Tena Clinic's Cardiology Department is equipped with advanced diagnostic and interventional technology to manage all aspects of heart health. Our cardiologists provide expert care for conditions ranging from hypertension and arrhythmia to heart failure and coronary artery disease.

    Heart Monitoring

    Continuous cardiac monitoring using Holter monitors and telemetry to detect arrhythmias and other heart rhythm abnormalities.

    ECG Analysis

    Detailed ECG interpretation and stress testing to evaluate heart function and identify ischemic heart disease and conduction abnormalities.

    Blood Tests

    Comprehensive blood panels including lipid profiles, cardiac enzymes, and coagulation studies to assess cardiovascular risk.

    Preventive Care

    Lifestyle counseling, medication management, and regular follow-up programs to prevent heart disease and manage existing conditions.

    Cardiology

    Comprehensive heart care with advanced diagnostic tools and expert cardiologists dedicated to your cardiovascular health.
